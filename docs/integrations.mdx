## Building your own integration

Here is how to build your own integration with ZBD:

- Read the documentation on [how to create payment requests](/api-reference/charges/create) and/or [how to make payments](/api-reference/charges/create).
- Integrate with your product offering.
- [Reach out to us](mailto:info@zebedee.io) to feature your product integration.
