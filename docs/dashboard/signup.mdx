---
title: 'Create Account'
description: 'Learn how to signup for your own ZBD Developer account.'
---

To create an account on ZBD today and integrate global instant payments in all of your projects. Follow the quick steps below:
### Access ZBD Dev Dashboard

- Navigate to the [ZBD Dev Dashboard](https://dashboard.zebedee.io/signup).

<Frame caption="ZBD Dev Dashboard | Sign Up">
  <img src="/img/v2/signup.png" alt="ZBD Dev Dashboard | Sign Up" />
</Frame>
<br />

### Enter Your Information

- Fill in the form with your information, then press `Create Account` to register.

<Frame caption="ZBD Dev Dashboard | Sign Up">
  <img src="/img/v2/signup-2.png" alt="ZBD Dev Dashboard | Sign Up" />
</Frame>
<br />

<Note>If you have an account on the ZBD app, you will need to use a **different email address** to create an account on the ZBD Dev Dashboard.</Note>  

### Verify Your Email

- After successful account creation, you should receive an email with a 6-digit verification code. Copy those digits and head on over to the dashboard to verify your email.

<Frame caption="ZBD Dev Dashboard | Verify Email">
  <img src="/img/v2/signup-verify-email.png" alt="ZBD Dev Dashboard | Verify Email" />
</Frame>
<br />

### You're set!

Now you can [complete your profile](/docs/dashboard/complete-profile) or begin making and receiving payments by [creating your first project](/docs/dashboard/projects/create).
