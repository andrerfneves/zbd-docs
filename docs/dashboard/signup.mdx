## Create Account[​](#create-account "Direct link to heading")

To create an account on ZEBEDEE today and integrate global instant payments in all of your projects. Follow the quick steps below:

1.  Navigate to the [ZEBEDEE Developer Dashboard](https://dashboard.zebedee.io/signup).

<Frame type="glass" caption="ZEBEDEE Developer Dashboard | Sign Up">
  <img src="/img/v2/signup.png" alt="ZEBEDEE Developer Dashboard | Sign Up" />
</Frame>
<br />

2.  Fill in your information, then press `Create Account` to register.

<Frame type="glass" caption="ZEBEDEE Developer Dashboard | Sign Up">
  <img src="/img/v2/signup-2.png" alt="ZEBEDEE Developer Dashboard | Sign Up" />
</Frame>
<br />

<Note>If you have an account on the ZBD app, you will need to use a **different email address** to create an account on the Developer Dashboard.</Note>  

3.  After successful account creation, you should receive an email with a 6-digit verification code. Copy those digits and head on over to the dashboard to verify your email.

<Frame type="glass" caption="ZEBEDEE Developer Dashboard | Verify Email">
  <img src="/img/v2/signup-verify-email.png" alt="ZEBEDEE Developer Dashboard | Verify Email" />
</Frame>
<br />

**That’s all there is to it!**

Now you can [complete your profile](/docs/dashboard/complete-profile) or begin making and receiving payments by [creating your first project](/docs/dashboard/projects/create).
