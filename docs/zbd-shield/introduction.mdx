ZBD Shield protects your game from bot farming and other malicious activity by helping you identify anomalous withdrawals and ban entities from transacting on the ZBD API. You can see ZBD Shield as a protective barrier defending your project from malicious actors through advanced network analysis that provides granular control over your project’s transaction activity.

This guide will walk you through how to set up your ZBD Shield instance to work with your game or application, answer FAQs, and outline steps for common actions.

### Prerequisites

Before you begin, you’ll need to have the following:

-  A [ZBD Dev Dashboard](https://dashboard.zebedee.io) Project
-  A ZBD Shield instance

**[Please contact us](mailto:info@zebedee.io) if you wish to purchase ZBD Shield instance(s).**

Once purchased, the ZBD Customer Success team will provide all the information you need, including login credentials to your unique ZBD Shield instance. Please have this information on-hand as you navigate this guide. If you do not have this information, please reach out to your Customer Success contact and we’ll promptly send it over.

Now let's get started!
