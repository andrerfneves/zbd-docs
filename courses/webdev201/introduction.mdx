## Course

The WebDev201 Course is meant to be the evolution point for developers hoping to learn more about development of web apps. It is a curated collection of video guides and tutorials that will introduce you to more advanced Web concepts, such as *data fetching from APIs*, *client-side state management*, *database queries*, and much more.

## Prerequisites

- [WebDev101 from zbd.dev](/courses/webdev101) completed
